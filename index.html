About

Wedding Project India is a wedding planner in Karungal, located in Kanyakumari. They offer end to end planning and execution services for all wedding-related events all over South India with a personalised theme. They also organise themed parties for birthdays to bachelorettes. Their wedding planning services include but are not limited to the selection of exquisite venues, curating invites, setting up the decor, styling up your family, providing premium catering service, sourcing the best photographers, providing varied entertainment options and arranging customised presents for the guests.


Services Offered

From pre-wedding to the post-wedding functions, the wedding is full of fun and mesmerising traditions, and they need individual planning and implementation as each carries a unique place. The team of wedding planners is experienced in arranging weddings and has excellent management and execution skills visible in the way they arrange your wedding ceremonies.
